Improved the tracking of the baaraku timesheet for candidates to automatically clock in and out of their shifts. The system with extension integrated to the platform uses a combination of browser extension and backend services to ensure accurate time tracking, even when candidates forget to manually clock in or out. The integration also includes notifications and reminders for candidates to maintain accurate records of their work hours which is crucial for payroll and performance evaluations.
